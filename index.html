First steps in designing a house?

Identify needs, set a budget, choose a location, and consult an architect.

How long to design a house?

A few weeks to several months or maybe a year

Cost to design a house?

Typically 5% to 15% of construction cost.

Key factors to consider?

Lifestyle, future needs, lot orientation, light, ventilation, and regulations.

Need a professional designer?

Recommended for functionality, aesthetics, and compliance.

Changes during construction?

Possible but can increase costs and cause delays.
